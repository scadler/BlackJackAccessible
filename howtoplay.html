How to Play

Each player is dealt 2 cards. Your cards are on the left while the dealer's are on the right. The objective of this game is to get as close as possible to 21 points without going over 21 points. Cards 2-10 are worth face value, face cards are worth 10 points, aces can be worth 1 or 11 points. To change the value of an ace click on it. A blackjack is when the first two cards that you are dealt are an ace along with any face card.

After "Deal" is clicked you are dealt two cards. When "Hit" is click you are dealt an additional card per click. A score that is more than 21 points results in an immediate loss. After "Stay" is clicked the dealer's turn starts.

If you beat the computer you get $5. If the dealer wins you lose $5. If you get a blackjack you win $10.
